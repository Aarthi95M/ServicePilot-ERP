ServicePilot — Privacy Policy

Field Service Management App

Last updated: June 2026

ServicePilot ("the App") is operated by Ecoriva ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use the ServicePilot mobile application.

1. Data We Collect

When you use ServicePilot, we collect:

  • Name, email address, and phone number (account registration)
  • Precise location data (GPS attendance check-in and job site verification)
  • Job records, attendance logs, and leave requests (operational data)
  • Device information for authentication and security purposes

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate your identity and manage your account
  • To record attendance based on your GPS location at shift start/end
  • To assign and track field service jobs
  • To generate operational reports for your employer
  • To send in-app notifications about your jobs and requests

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your data is only accessible to:

  • Your employer (the company that deployed ServicePilot)
  • Authorised administrators and supervisors within your organisation
  • Our hosting infrastructure providers — data is encrypted in transit and at rest

4. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active or as required by your employer's operational and legal requirements. You may request deletion at any time.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal data

To exercise these rights, contact your company administrator or email us directly at aarthu95@gmail.com.

6. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS with JWT-based authentication. We apply industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, disclosure, or loss.

7. Location Data

Location access is used solely for GPS attendance verification (check-in/check-out) and job site confirmation. Location data is not shared with third parties and is only visible to authorised personnel within your organisation.

8. Children's Privacy

ServicePilot is intended for use by adults (18+) in a professional workplace context. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
